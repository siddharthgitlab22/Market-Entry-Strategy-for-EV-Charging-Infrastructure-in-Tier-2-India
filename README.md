## рџ“Њ Overview

India's electric vehicle (EV) ecosystem is experiencing rapid growth, creating significant opportunities for charging infrastructure providers. This project evaluates how **Uber** can strategically enter India's EV charging market by identifying high-potential Tier-2 cities, estimating market opportunities, analyzing competitors, and recommending an execution roadmap.

The study focuses on **Surat, Jaipur, and Lucknow** using structured consulting frameworks and market research.

---

# рџЋЇ Problem Statement

As EV adoption accelerates, Uber has the opportunity to expand beyond mobility services into charging infrastructure.

This project answers the following business questions:

- Which Tier-2 cities should Uber enter first?
- How large is the addressable market?
- What business model should Uber adopt?
- Who are the target customers?
- What are the competitive challenges?
- How should Uber execute its expansion?
